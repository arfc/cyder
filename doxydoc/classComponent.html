Detailed Description

Defines interface for subcomponents of the Cyder generic repository.

Components such as the Waste Form, Waste Package, Buffer, Near Field, Far Field, and Envrionment will share a universal interface so that information passing concerning fluxes and other boundary conditions can be passed in and out of them.
